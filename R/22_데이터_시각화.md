AStore <- c(20, 21,23,22,26,28,35,35,41,42,43,45,44,45,46,47,47,46,47,58,59,60,56,57,57,80)

- points() : 한 지점을 포인트로 표시해줌

```R
boxplot(AStore, new.B, CStore, names=c("A",'B','C'))
points(c(mean(AStore), mean(new.B), mean(CStore)),
       pch=3, col='red',cex=2)
```
